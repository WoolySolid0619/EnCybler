✅ Internal and External Cleaning

We clean your computer inside and out to reduce dust, improve airflow, and prevent overheating.

🧼 What does the cleaning include?

External cleaning:

  • ✔️ Keyboard, screen, case, and vents.
  • ✔️ USB ports, HDMI, accessible fans.
  • ✔️ Anti-static products and specialized cloths are used.

Internal cleaning:

  • 🔧 Safe opening of the device (laptop or PC).
  • 🌀 Dust removal from fans, motherboard, and heatsinks.
  • ❄️ Cooling inspection and thermal paste replacement if necessary.

⚠️ Why is it important?

  • 🔥 Dust causes overheating.
  • 🐢 A dirty computer is slower and noisier.
  • 💥 Avoid long-term damage to sensitive components.
  • 🎧 Work quietly and with better performance.

🔁 Recommended Frequency

✅ Every 6 to 12 months for regular use.
🐶 If you have pets or a dusty environment: every 4 to 6 months.
